Welcome to Quebaan Professional Services

We are seasoned professionals providing top quality services in the areas of accounting, taxation and business advisory with practical experience of over three decades in both the private and public sectors of the Nigerian economy.

Our Objectives are to:

  1. use our professional expertise in the area of accounting, audit, investigation, taxation and management for creative business solutions.
  2. continually strive to be your dependable ally in handling all your regulatory and compliance requirements in taxation, audit, corporate restructuring, insolvency, investments and divestments decisions.
  3. use our vast knowledge and experience in accounting, audit, taxation and management in training and retraining of your staff to equip them with the skills and knowledge necessary to perform their duties effectively and efficiently.

Our Vision

To be a point of reference for strategic decisions in accounting, auditing and taxation for Government Agencies, Large and Medium Enterprises - Local and International.

Our Mission

To optimize value addition in all our services to our clients.

Our Values

  1. Professionalism and High Ethical Standards.
  2. Efficient Service Delivery
  3. Value Addition through continuous improvement.
